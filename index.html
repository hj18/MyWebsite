More about me

My name is Haia Jihan, I'm a third year computer science student at Heriot-Watt University in Edinburgh. I’ve been a gamer since a young age and always dreamed to create games which players can enjoy.
Video games are my passion. Games such as Dishonored 2 and Prey, made me curious about the thinking process involved in level design and the way programmers are involved in all this.
I love gameplay design in games such as Prey give players freedom to reach the destination in so many ways, making players think about their actions, this always amazes me because I keep asking myself how they managed that.
My favourite gaming genres are RPG, games such as Divinity Original Sin 2, Fallout: New Vegas and many more. These games enable me to explore and be someone else.
I’m also a massive fan of Metroidvania, especially Blasphemous, Super Metroid and Castlevania: Symphony of the Night. In every game the map amazes me, and I try to create my own.
During my second year I worked as far of a team to creat an online multiplayer game I was responsible for managing the team, I also worked on HTML, CSS, some JavaScriple as well and helped with the character design. As of now, I'm in my third year learning subjects such as AI which is a big part of video games. In my free time I'm working on mini projects to build my portfolio. I also enjoy reading fantasy books and playing the flute. I am confident that being part of this internship will help me develop my skills and learn more new and wonderful programming techniques.
